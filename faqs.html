FAQs

1. How many categories are involved?

There are just three (3) categories
Which are:
1. Music
2. Acting/Comedy and
3. Dance
Other category such as Writing will be welcomed as well. So if you are a writer (music, movie or other) you can also register for the competition.

2. Is there going to be over all winner?

No. There's going to be grand winner for each category and 1st & 2nd runner up for all categories.

3. When is the deadline for registration?

April 4th 2021

4. When is the competition going to start?

April 30th 2021

5. How long is the competition going to past?

Two (2) months and two (2) weeks.

6. When and where will the Awards night take place?

The competition is going end on Sunday 18th of July 2021 @11:59pm.
And the Awards night presentation of prizes will be on the 15th of August 2021 @2pm.
Location is Magodo Shangisha Lagos.
Full address will be sent to everyone that participated in the competition.

7. How can I register?

The registration process is very easy and simple.
Please click here to the step-by-step guide on how to register.

Note:

: In as much we've made this opportunity a free entry competition, be aware that voting cost just N50 per vote.
Don't worry you will get informations and help on how to make people vote for you or give you money to vote for yourself.
