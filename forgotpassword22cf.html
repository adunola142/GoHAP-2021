upcomming artists

The goal of this contest is to find an outstanding performer, band, singer, or solo artist who has yet to sign a record deal. The rules of this competition are also very beneficial to the amateur musician; for one, anyone can enter, and they can submit as many songs or pieces of music as they like. Register on gohapac2021 and see how seamless communication can be. We will automatically top up your airtime once it gets to a particular limit. Buying airtime just got interesting!

graphic designers

In this competition, we welcome participants to explore their craft of creating visual content to convey a powerful message related to current issues through enhanced visual interpretation. Participants can create a new content for this competition and also can submit their previous work produced after 1st January 2021 which focuses on design as a primary visual element. Participants need to submit the solutions along with a brief write up describing their work which should not be more than 150 words.

ABOUT GOHAPAC

WHAT IS GOHAPAC 2021?

This means; Gospel Hip-hop and Afrobeat/Pop Artists Competition.
DiSEmpire.It is an online contest/competition
to help bring young adults and youths to a level in their career in the creative industries.
focused more on Gospel Hip hop and Afrobeat/pop music artists,
Positive/Educative/Informative content creators.

We are not just letting you compete for the prize, we are also bringing you the best of trainings that will help you earn good money from your creativity and become financially free in few months.
